About

Service

CKI is a leadership based organization that empowers college students to develop into responsible leaders with a lifelong commitment to service.

CKI takes part in many activities, including:

  • Community Service Projects - Local volunteer activities like food drives and clean-ups to support community needs.
  • Fundraising Initiativess - Events to raise funds for local charities and global causes.
  • Leadership Development - Workshops and conferences to enhance leadership skills and teamwork.
  • Outreach - International projects that promote cultural understanding and address global issues.
  • Fellowship and Networking - Social events that connect members and foster a supportive community.
